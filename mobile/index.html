Microphone Permission Required

BlueFlow needs access to your microphone to stream audio to your PC.

How to enable microphone:

1
Tap the lock icon 🔒 or ⓘ in the address bar
2
Tap "Permissions" or "Site settings"
3
Set Microphone to "Allow"
4
Come back and tap the mic button again
1
Open Settings → Safari → Microphone and set to "Allow"
ℹ️
Open your browser settings → Site permissions → Microphone → set to Allow for this site
⚙️
Also check: Android Settings → Apps → [Your Browser] → Permissions → Microphone → Allow
